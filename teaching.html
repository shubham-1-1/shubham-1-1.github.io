ASL380: Climate Modeling [3-0-0-3]

The course introduces the physical foundations of the climate system and the principles of climate model construction. The course emphasizes conservation laws, energy transfer, and large-scale wave dynamics.

Key Topics

  • Climate system & conservation laws
  • Numerical modeling & parameterization
  • Model simulation, tuning & validation
  • Atmospheric & wave processes

Learning Approach

The course integrates theory with case-based numerical experiments. Students conduct studies, including topographic forcing of circulation and CO₂-doubling climate response using the NCAR Community Atmosphere Model (CAM).
