Engagements

Building Capacity for Climate-Resilient Futures

For Students

Internships (Minimum 6 Months)

I host research interns for a minimum duration of six months to enable meaningful engagement with ongoing research. Interns work closely within active research themes and contribute substantively to scholarly outputs. Short-term observational internships are generally not feasible due to the depth of work involved.

B.Tech Projects (IIT Delhi)

I supervise six-month B.Tech projects aligned with the research themes listed under Foci. Students are encouraged to approach with a strong analytical foundation and a genuine interest in research-oriented problem solving.

Ph.D. Supervision

I welcome doctoral students whose interests align with the research areas outlined in Foci, particularly in:

  • Monsoon dynamics
  • Climate model development and diagnostics
  • Regional climate change
  • Structural climate risk and systemic challenges

Prospective students should have solid quantitative preparation and a clear motivation for pursuing research in these areas. I value intellectual curiosity, rigor, and long-term commitment to scientific inquiry.

For Industry

Advisory and Consulting

I work with corporate stakeholders as an advisor for long-term strategic input or as a consultant for targeted scientific guidance. All engagements focus on where scientific insight contributes substantively to long-term outcomes.

For Government

Committees and Review

I participate in expert committees for government agencies, offering guidance on policy review, evaluation, and strategy formulation. I prioritize work where expertise informs systemic decisions.

Engagement Philosophy

I participate very selectively in engagements, focusing on opportunities where my expertise can make a meaningful contribution and support long-term, constructive outcomes. I generally avoid routine administrative meetings, large gatherings, and ceremonial events.
